About Me

Hi there! My name is Megan Zhang.

I am a student at UNC Chapel Hill majoring in Computer Science and Communications. Although I have decided to pursue a different career path, I have always had a passion for the arts. When I'm not working or studying for class, I like to spend my time painting. My favorite medium is acrylic paint. I am lucky to have had the opportunity to make custom pieces for many people throughout the years. This page serves as a platform for me to showcase the work I have done.

Get In Touch

How It Works

If you have an item in mind that you would like me to customize, reach out to me with the design details and I will get back to you as soon as I can. I can paint on denim and leather jackets, shoes, hats, and other apparel. You may choose to ship an item that you already own or have a new purchase delivered to my address. I have also done many photo-recreational canvas paintings. If there are any miscellaneous items you would like me to customize, simply let me know and I will see if it can be done! Pinterest and Google Images are great sources of inspiration for designs, so feel free to attach any reference photos in your email. Once the item and design have been established, I will provide a pricing quote. I hope to hear from you soon!
